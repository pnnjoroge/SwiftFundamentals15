
			UIVIEW OBJECT

- What are three of the primary responsibilities of a UIView object?
	1. To draw content and animate view properties to new values
	2. To adjust the size and position of subviews
	3. To respond to different types of events such as touch and gestures
- What does documentation call a view that's embedded in another view?
	Subview
- What does documentation call the parent view that's embedding the other view?
	Superview
- What is a view's frame? 
	The origin and dimensions of the view in the coordinate system of its superview
- How is a view's bounds different from its frame?
	Bounds defines the internal dimensions of the view as it sees them, making the frame an 	external property vs bounds an internal property
